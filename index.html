About

I got birth in Malaysia and graduated from National Taiwan University which is the top university in Taiwan. I majored in Agricultural Chemistry enabling me not only the knowledge of chemistry and biotechnology but an effective and logical concept from more than 200 experiments. In fact, I am highly interested and passionate in business and therefore took several subjects such as Macroeconomics, Investment, Money & Banking, and etc. With both Chemical and Business background, comparing to the other candidates, I am capable with thinking differently.

I served as the president of NTU Ballroom Dancing Club which had about 200 members. When I was a chairman, our team made a most-member record that hasn’t been broken yet. Within 3 years in dance-teaching group and almost 100 teaching classes; moreover 1 year as a president and held many performances and activities, all these experiences had been strengthen my skill of administration, problem-solving and negotiation. Furthermore, I learned something essential for a team-leader as team-player are optimistic, courage, listening.

“I have done something that most people can’t do!” This was what I told myself as achieving my first 42km marathon. “Marathon is the perfect distance to truly test the human physiology and spirit,” said Frank Shorter, the gold medal in the Olympic marathon. This saying indicates that Persistence is the key to goal and it is what I temper for.

Being a chairman, achieving marathons, what I do is that I believe in CHALLENGEING THE QUO.
