About 元

I am a UX & Visual Designer with over 8 years of experience developing desktop, mobile and web applications using Lean UX and Agile design frameworks. My work emphasizes a human-centered approach to design that weds a holistic understanding of the design process with skills spanning from concept generation and development, through front-end coding, prototyping, and user testing. I am curious about the relationship between the products I help manifest and the users who interact with them; and draw satisfaction that the tools I’ve helped create have in turn helped millions of people express their own creativity and impact the world.

I was born and raised in a small rural village in Taiwan where I excelled academically, but let a relatively sheltered life. Five years ago I moved to the US for graduate school and the culture shock I experienced inspired a deep curiosity about the people, places and cultures that make up our world. Since arriving in the US, I’ve traveled to numerous countries on six continents. I love meeting people and learning their stories and I am currently working with a publisher in Taiwan to write a book about my adventures. I hope to inspire other native Chinese speakers to push their comfort levels and question their assumptions. I also enjoy art, music, and photography.

元 (Yuan-Yu), is my Chinese name, which was created by my mother when I was born. It combines concepts of originality and uniqueness with a sense of motion.
